02·

Tap the CTA on the card. Short intake form — two to four minutes. Describe what you're working on. Pay the Stripe link when scope confirms. No discovery calls unless the offer is one.

03·

Inside the SLA — 24 hours, 48 hours, 7 days depending on the shadow. If we slip the SLA, next delivery is free. Every delivery includes a note on which other shadow might help next. No pressure.
