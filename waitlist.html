What We're Building

LUX Seeds

A LUX Seed is a pre-built cognitive architecture package -- a configured set of AI entities tuned for your specific context. Each entity has a role, a voice, a memory system, and a set of tools. Together they form a persistent field that thinks with you, not just for you.

This is not a chatbot. It is not a single model you prompt and forget. A LUX Seed is an operating system for your mind -- a living cognitive layer that runs in the background, routes your thinking, holds your memory, and speaks in voices tuned to how you actually work.
